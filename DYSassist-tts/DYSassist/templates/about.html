About DYSassist

"Read with Ease"

Our Mission

DYSassist is dedicated to making reading accessible to everyone by converting documents into audiobooks. We understand that everyone learns differently, and for some, traditional reading can be challenging. That's why we've created a simple, user-friendly platform to convert your documents into audio format.

What We Do

Our application allows users to:

  • Upload PDF and DOCX documents
  • Convert text documents into high-quality audio
  • Access your audiobooks anywhere, anytime
  • Manage your document library easily

How It Works

Simply upload your document, and our system will convert it into clear, natural-sounding audio. Perfect for students, professionals, and anyone who prefers audio learning.
